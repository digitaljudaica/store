Law 6

Also, if the maslul was three hindred degrees, subtract it from three hundred and sixty - and sixty will remain. And you already know that parallax correction for sixty degrees [is] one degree and fouty one minutes, and so is the parallax correction for three hundred degrees. And the same way - for every number.
