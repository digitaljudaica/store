Law 3

A correction that depends on the doubled distance is added to the mean lunar anomaly, giving corrected lunar anomaly (מסלול הנכון):

doubled distance correction of the mean anomaly

6°-11° 1°

39°-45° 6°
46°-51° 7°
52°-59° 8°
