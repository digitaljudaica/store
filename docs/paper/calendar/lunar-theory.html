Law 4,5,6: True Lunar Longitude.

Formulae:

\[\tan ^{-1} \left({\sin \alpha \over \cos \alpha + \epsilon} \right)\]

\(R\) - radius of the big circle; \(r\) - radius of the small circle; \(\epsilon = R/r\). A little trigonometry, and we get: visible anomaly = arctg(sin(corrected anomaly)/(cos(corrected anomaly) + e)) and: e = ctg(visible anomaly)*sin(corrected anomaly) - cos(corrected anomaly)
